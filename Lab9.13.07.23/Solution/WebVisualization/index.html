3D Map

This 3D Map Render is for the Lab9 Exercise on Delineation of the streams in Dzaleka Camp in Lilongwe Malawi


This shows the 4 main stream bodies and gives a vivid description of how each of them occupies the areas surrounding it and also the locations of where the streams are going and where they are coming from.
